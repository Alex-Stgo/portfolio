The Economic Forecast Report is usually used as a guide to determine the strategy, budgets and plans for upcoming years. Government officials and business managers use economic forecasts to determine financial and operative policies according to the future cashflows.
The current report was built to automatically connect with The Mexican Central Bank's economists expectations to generate a month-to-month report.

Details

Scope

Many companies usually require to perform forecastings every quarter to adjust cashflows expectations. This report consumes information directly from the API exposed by the Economic Information System of Banxico. It provides historical financial and economic information and perspectives for the following months which are going to be consumed to achieve the main goal. With this information, the report will estimate rates such as the interbank equilibrium interest rate and the treasury certificates interest rate for the following year performing a five-year linear regression with the daily bank funding rate as a regressor.

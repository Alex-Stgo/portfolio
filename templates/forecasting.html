The Economic Forecast Report is usually used as a guide to determine the strategy, budgets and plans for upcoming years. Government officials and business managers use economic forecasts to determine financial and operative policies according to the future cashflows.
The current report was built to automatically connect with The Central Bank' economists expectations to disaggreagate the information to a month-to-month overview.

Details

Scope

The report was intented to consume information from the Central Bank Portal: www.banxico.org.mx, process it and present it in a relevant structure to allow the balance sheet and income statement to be estimated for the following year.
Banxico is considered as a reliable benchmark for the mexican market expectations. Banxico usually releases this information every end of month, collecting estimations from around 40 economists and providing the statistics related to their opinions.
The most important estimation from Banxico, related to the current report, is the daily bank funding rate. As a prior analysis, the correlation between the daily bank funding rate and other mexican rates (such as the interbank equilibrium interest rate and the treasury certificates interest rate) was analyzed. The correlation for the rates to be estimated was above 0.95.

Implementation

Given the previous results, the task consists basically in extract historical information to calculate the coeficients related to the regression to obtain the forecasted values for the rates in the scope. The report was built in a Excel worksheet using the Power Query functionality. It is important to highlight that Power Query and Power BI rely on M code to program the data transformations.

Banxico has built and exposed its own API service to be consumed for the final users. The details about the API could be found here.
The request construction is as follows:

https://www.banxico.org.mx/SieAPIRest/service/v1/series/:SerieID/datos/:StartDate/:EndDate
